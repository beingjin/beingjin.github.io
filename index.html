Notes on State of Mind

3 November 2018

So, I broke up with Chris - finally, really. Thanks to being at the right time in my hormonal cycle, I was less emotional and, thanks to having had the time to really process my thoughts and state of mind, I managed to be firm. Also, I think I just felt generally less close or romantic towards him. Still felt sad about leaving him alone while he is going through a very trying time in his life. It's hard to watch him feel so down and, as a coping mechanism, put on this cynical face to conceal his crushing heart.

But I had to. I began to feel both lonely and trapped somehow, as it practically made me unavailable. And my mind constantly wandered and looked for someone. That elusive someone.

Now I am reviewing, re-honing, my HTML skills at Starbucks near Southwark, after visiting Tate Modern. Remembering, there are still things I can enjoy and do for my own life and happiness. Just miserating alone wouldn't change anything and I am still shining like a star.
